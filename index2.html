Dr. Joe Dispenza Biography

Dr. Joe Dispenza is an American neuroscientist, chiropractor, international lecturer, researcher, corporate consultant, author, and educator born and brought up in California, United States. Additionally, Dispenza is a faculty member at Quantum University in Honolulu, Hawaii; the Omega Institute for Holistic Studies in Rhinebeck, New York; and Kripalu Center for Yoga and Health in Stockbridge, Massachusetts.

How old is Dr. Joe Dispenza?

Joe Dispenza is 58 years old as of 2020. He was born on March 22nd, 1962 in California, United States. He celebrates his birthday every March 22nd of every year. His birth sign is Pisces.

Dr. Joe Dispenza Education

Dispenza holds a Bachelor’s degree from The Evergreen State College in Olympia, Washington. Additionally, Joe also holds a Doctor of Chiropractic Degree from Life University in Atlanta, Georgia

Dr. Joe Dispenza Accident

In 1986, Dispenza had an accident when he was run over by an SUV. At the time, Dispenza was competing in a triathlon event in Palm Springs, California. Although Joe had broken six vertebrae, he chose philosophical inner healing rather than have high-risk surgeries. After the accident, Joe authored the book,'‘You are the Placebo’

Dr. Joe Dispenza Meditation

Dispenza recommends meditation as a healing process for people to heal and also evolve their consciousness. In addition to that, Joe bases his meditation on his findings from neuroscience, epigenetics, and quantum physics. Apart from that, Joe has partnered with other scientists to research on the effects of meditation. Dispenza researches epigenetic testing, brain mapping with electroencephalograms (EEGs), and individual energy field testing with a gas discharge visualization (GDV) machine.
